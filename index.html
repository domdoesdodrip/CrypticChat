Learn How to Use CrypticChat

1

Create an Account

Open the app and click Sign Up. Pick a username and password. After you click Register, click Login to enter.

2

Pick your Alias

Inside the app, enter an Alias. This is the name people will see in the chat.

3

Choose a Room

The Room ID field determines your frequency.

Public Chat

Leave blank for Global Room.

Private Chat

Enter a secret ID for hidden chats.

4

Leave Safely

Click the Disconnect icon. Closing the tab without disconnecting may leave a "ghost" for 30 seconds.
